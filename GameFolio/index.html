GameFolio

Short Pitch

An easily accessible website where users can share what they’ve made and keep all their collected work in a single website.

Long Pitch

The website will allow users to make their own profile to host their portfolio online. Allowing them to share their game with others who also share the hobby and allowing potential employers to find a potential candidates portfolios online. Collaboration would be encourage, giving both users the ability to post the game under each other’s names, and potential linking with Linkedin to make users more connected with their professional social media.
